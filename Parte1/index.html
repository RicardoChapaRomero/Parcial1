Tribute to Stephen Hawking

"Nothing is better than reading and gaining more and more knowledge".

TIMELINE

  • Jan. 8, 1942: Stephen Hawking is born in Oxford, England.

  • Jul. 14, 1965: Marries his wife Jane Wilde.

  • 1988: Publishes "A Brief History of Time", a book on cosmology aimed for the general public.

  • Mar. 14, 2018: Stephen Hawking dies at age of 76 in Cambridge, UK.
